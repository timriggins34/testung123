What is MOOWR?

The Manufacture and Other Operations in Warehouse (MOOWR) scheme under Section 65 of the Customs Act is a revolutionary initiative supporting the "Make in India" drive. It allows manufacturers to import capital goods and raw materials at zero upfront customs duty by storing them in a bonded warehouse.

If the final manufactured product is exported, the deferred duty is completely waived. If sold in the domestic market, the duty on the imported inputs is paid only at the time of clearance from the warehouse.

Transformative Benefits

  • ✔ No Export Obligation: Unlike EPCG or Advance Licences, MOOWR does not force you to meet rigid export targets to enjoy duty benefits.
  • ✔ Infinite Deferment: Capital goods can be used indefinitely in the warehouse without ever paying the customs duty, provided they aren't cleared into the domestic market.
  • ✔ Cash Flow Optimization: Eliminates massive upfront tax costs on heavy machinery imports, freeing up working capital.
  • ✔ Seamless Domestic Sales: Flexibility to sell manufactured goods in the Domestic Tariff Area (DTA) by simply paying duty on the inputs consumed.

Key Documents Required

  • Detailed Project Report & Manufacturing Process Flow
  • Site layout and floor plan of the premises
  • IEC, PAN, and GST Registration
  • List of capital goods/raw materials to be imported
  • Ownership proof or lease agreement for the premises
  • Fire safety and standard municipal NOCs
  • Financial records & Solvency proof
  • General Bond Undertaking (Section 59)
