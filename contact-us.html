Our Core Practice Areas

Customs Litigation

Expert representation in matters involving classification disputes, valuation challenges, SVB finalization, confiscation of goods, and drafting replies to Show Cause Notices (SCN) under the Customs Act.

GST Advisory & Disputes

Comprehensive Goods and Services Tax consultancy. We handle refund claims, audit responses, ITC disputes, advance rulings, and litigation before GST Appellate Authorities and Tribunals.

Excise & Service Tax

Resolution of legacy tax disputes. We offer strategic litigation support for pending Central Excise and Service Tax cases, ensuring compliance and representation through the appellate process.

DGFT & Foreign Trade

Advisory on Foreign Trade Policy (FTP) benefits. We represent clients in matters regarding EPCG/Advance Authorization defaults, adjudication proceedings, and appeals before the Directorate General of Foreign Trade.

FEMA Compliance

Foreign Exchange Management Act advisory. We guide businesses through cross-border transaction regulations, compounding of contraventions, and RBI compliance for import/export dealings.

PMLA Matters

Prevention of Money Laundering Act defense and advisory. We provide robust legal representation and strategic counsel during investigations, attachments, and proceedings under PMLA provisions.
