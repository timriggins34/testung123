Certifications & Licences

Expert consultancy to help you capitalize on Foreign Trade Policies and duty exemptions.

DGFT & Regulatory Consultancy

Navigating the Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT) and Customs regulations requires specialized knowledge. We help businesses procure necessary licenses, ensuring you receive the maximum benefits and compliance under current policies.

EPCG & Advance Licences →

We assist in procuring and redeeming Export Promotion Capital Goods (EPCG) licenses and Advance Authorizations, allowing you to import capital goods and raw materials at zero customs duty based on export obligations.

EPR Certification →

Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) compliance is now mandatory for plastic packaging, e-waste, and battery importers/manufacturers. We facilitate your CPCB registration and target fulfillment seamlessly.

AEO Certification →

Authorized Economic Operator (AEO) status grants your business faster customs clearances, deferred payment of duties, and direct port delivery. We guide you through the complex application and audit process.

SVB Registration →

If you are importing goods from related parties or foreign parent companies, Special Valuation Branch (SVB) procedures are mandatory. We handle questionnaires and representations to prevent unnecessary duty loading.

MOOWR Scheme →

Manufacture and Other Operations in Warehouse (MOOWR) enables duty deferment on imported capital goods and inputs used for domestic manufacturing. We handle the application, bonding, and compliance.

Self-Sealing Permission →

Empower your export operations with factory stuffing permissions. We help secure customs approval for self-sealing of export containers using RFID seals, saving you time and avoiding port congestion.

Other Certifications →

Need allied agency clearances? We assist with FSSAI, BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards), WPC (Wireless Planning & Coordination), Plant Quarantine (PQ), Animal Quarantine (AQCS), and more.
