Navigating Participating Government Agencies (PGAs)

Importing and exporting regulated commodities requires specific No Objection Certificates (NOC) from Indian government bodies. Our regulatory team coordinates directly with these agencies to ensure your goods meet all standards and clear customs without costly holding delays.

FSSAI Clearance

Mandatory for food products, supplements, and additives. We assist with product approval, labeling compliance checks, and securing the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India NOC.

BIS Registration

For electronics, IT goods, toys, steel, and chemicals. We guide you through the Bureau of Indian Standards testing, Compulsory Registration Scheme (CRS), and ISI mark certification.

WPC ETA Approval

Equipment Type Approval (ETA) from the Wireless Planning & Coordination wing for devices operating in de-licensed frequency bands (like Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, and RFID equipment).

Plant & Animal Quarantine

Phytosanitary certificates and NOCs from PQ (Plant Quarantine) and AQCS (Animal Quarantine) for importing agricultural products, timber, seeds, and animal-origin goods.

CDSCO & ADC

Central Drugs Standard Control Organisation (CDSCO) and Assistant Drug Controller (ADC) clearances for importing pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and medical devices.

Legal Metrology (LMPC)

Expert assistance with Legal Metrology Packaged Commodities (LMPC) registration, which is strictly mandatory for importers bringing in pre-packaged goods meant for retail sale.
