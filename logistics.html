EPCG & Advance Licences

Maximize your export competitiveness with DGFT's zero-duty import schemes.

What are these Schemes?

The Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT) offers powerful incentive schemes to boost India's exports. Advance Authorisation (AA) allows duty-free import of raw materials and inputs required for manufacturing export products. The Export Promotion Capital Goods (EPCG) scheme allows the import of capital goods (machinery) at zero customs duty, subject to an export obligation equal to 6 times the duty saved.

Key Benefits

  • ✔ Zero Import Duty: Completely waive Basic Customs Duty, IGST, and other cesses on eligible imports.
  • ✔ Pricing Edge: Lowering your input and capital costs directly enhances your pricing competitiveness globally.
  • ✔ Tech Upgradation: EPCG makes it affordable to import state-of-the-art machinery to modernize your manufacturing.
  • ✔ Domestic Sourcing: These authorizations can also be used to source inputs domestically without paying terminal excise duties.

Documents Required for Application

Applying for these authorizations requires precise documentation. Our team ensures your files are perfectly aligned with DGFT norms before submission.

  • Import Export Code (IEC)
  • Digital Signature Certificate (Class 3)
  • RCMC (Registration-cum-Membership Certificate) from relevant Export Promotion Council
  • Proforma Invoice of the goods to be imported
  • MSME / Udyog Aadhaar Registration (if applicable)
  • Chartered Accountant (CA) Certificate
  • Detailed Nexus declaration (showing the input-output norm or machinery-to-export relationship)
  • Previous export performance records
